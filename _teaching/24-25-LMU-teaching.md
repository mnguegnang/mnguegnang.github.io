At LMU, I taught topics at the intersection of non-convex optimization, machine learning, and deep learning. My responsibilities included designing exercise sheets, leading tutorials, and grading exams.

## Mathematical Foundations of Machine Learning II - Deep Learning

*   *Winter Semester (Oct 2024 – Mar 2025)*
*   **Key Topics:** Backpropagation calculus, stochastic gradient descent (SGD) dynamics, universal approximation theorems, deep network approximation, Barron class approximation, generalization, optimization, wide networks, and the neural tangent kernel.

## Optimization
*   *Summer Semester (Apr 2024 – Jul 2024)*
*   **Key Topics:** First and second order methods for unconstrained optimization, including gradient descent, Newton’s method, line search strategies, convergence rates of first-order methods, and constrained optimization.

  **Curriculum Design:** Translated complex theoretical proofs, such as global convergence of SGD, into accessible tutorials for students from diverse backgrounds.
